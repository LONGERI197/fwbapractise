FARM WITH BISASE AFRICA

About Us

Farm with Bisase Africa is an organization focusing and highlighting Agriculture, Food Security and Rural Development issues in Kenya. Besides, we farm underutilized crops like arrowroots, sweet potatoes, and indigenous vegetables. Our vision is to cover stories on Agriculture and Food Systems across the continent that will change the perspectives about Hunger, Poverty and Food Security in Africa.
